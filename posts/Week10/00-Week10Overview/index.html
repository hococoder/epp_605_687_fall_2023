Welcome to Week 10! This week we'll take a look at a few different iOS frameworks - Biometrics, for logging in, and ResearchKit for making your app capable of collecting research-grade information!

  • Biometrics - learn how to enabled FaceID (or Touch ID) in your app
  • Introduction to ResearchKit - learn how to built research-grade questionnaires, activities and plots in your app
  • Programming Assignment 10 - in this assignment, you'll apply these technologies to your app

There is NOT a Week10_Starter or Week10_Final project for this week; example code can be found in the EPExampleApp project in the resource bundle. There is, as usual, a starter project for the homework
