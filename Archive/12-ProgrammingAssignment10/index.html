Getting Ready

In your tenth programming assignment, you will add a year end survey for the student to fill out using ResearchKit. To get start, grab the starter project from the downloads file:

Homework 10 Starter (link will be active start of week 10)

Assignment Requirements

For this week's homework:

  • In the About view, add a link to take a ResearchKit based survey. This should ask the user a series of questions about what they thought of the class. You must:
    • Save your data to the disk when you are done.
    • Feel free to make up your own questions, and please use 3 different types of questions (at least) in your survey.

I've included ResearchKit in the starter project, but be sure to drag the xcodeproj file into your project and build it so you can use it!

Assignment Submission

  • Zip up the Xcode project folder. This should be the folder that has the .xcodeproj file inside it.
  • Rename the zip to LastName_Homework#.zip
  • Upload the zip to the Programming Assignment 10 area on Blackboard BEFORE 11:59 PM ET on the day it is due (Sunday of Week 10).
  • Be sure to test your app before submitting, so you know it works as intended!

Assignment Rubrics

In addition to the normal grading criteria stated in the syllabus, you will be graded on:

  • Proper use of the ResearchKit survey task
